Ohio State's annual tech innovation and entrepreneurship showcase.
Thursday, April 13, 2023 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Hosted in person this year at the Nationwide & Ohio Farm Bureau 4-H Center

This showcase highlights outstanding and innovative technologies developed by Ohio State faculty, students, and alumni. ShowOHI/O brings together invested parties from across Buckeye Nation for an evening that celebrates and supports tech entrepreneurs making an impact.

Event Information

Join us Thursday, April 13, 2023 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM this year as we return to hosting ShowOHI/O in person at the Nationwide & Ohio Farm Bureau 4-H Center! Enjoy food and beverages while checking out students, staff, faculty, and alumni who will be sharing their developing or improving technologies! The dress code will be business formal. All food and beverages will be included.

Awards

Buckeye Choice Award

Congratulations to last year's Buckeye Choice winners!

Last year's Buckeye Choice Winner was Opendemia! This team included Zac Graber. Opendemia is a website designed by students, for students, to make research and writing both quicker and easier so they can make a larger impact.

Last year's Runner Up was The Cool Beans! This team of presenters included Sawyer Ramsey (Computer Science & Engineering), Garrett Nerone (Industrial Systems Engineering), Bret Shafer (Computer Science & Engineering), and Hayden Miller (Mechanical Engineering). Their project was a smart door that opens with facial recognition and has wireless capabilities.

The Buckeye Choice Award is presented to a presenter who is chosen by the attendees.

The Buckeye Choice Award is sponsored by AEP and supported by the College of Engineering Advancement and Alumni Relations.
